Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Tense and relax each muscle group in your body, starting from your toes and working your way up to your head. This helps release physical tension.

Guided Visualization

Close your eyes and imagine a peaceful place, such as a beach or forest. Focus on the sights, sounds, and smells to calm your mind.
